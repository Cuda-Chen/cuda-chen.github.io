## Introduction

We rely on many Internet services for our work and living nowadays,
and there is no exception of LLM service such as Claude Code, ChatGPT,
Gemini, and so on. For most Internet services, you are required to create
an account with leaving some personal information such as e-mail, which
becomes a privacy threat as any information might make a connection
to yourself IRL. What's more, your account sometimes get banned because
the service provider such as Google considers you violate ToS, thus
you can no logner access any other services with the banned account.

For the most secure way to prevent your account and privacy is not
to register any LLM services. Nevertheless, it is usually not
feasible unless you have any alternatives for living. So, I would like
to propose an alternative: use a dedicated e-mail for registering any LLM services.
This is a less but much more feasible way to protect your privacy
and not being banned by accessing any other services even your account
becomes suspicious. To persuade you to do this, I would like to share
some aspects so that you will agree with me after reading this post.

## Preventing Bans by Service Providers 

You can register many LLM service providers using a third-party service
(for Google will become an exception: you need a Google account). However,
doing so will let you have the risk of total loss of data and accessibility
when your third-party service account get banned. What's worse, you are not able
to access any other services: taking Google as example, the banned account will
no longer to access any services of Google.

This becomes not possible to use a dedicated e-mail: the service providers
only blocks the e-mail but not your third-party account, making less
harm to yourself.

## Defeating Shadow Profiling

Many LLM services do data collection, including profiling, when you
use their services. By using a dedicated e-mail, you create an alternative
idendity which leaks less information of yourself, protecting for your
privacy.

## Containing the "Blast Radius"

Internet services face cyberattacks a lot, and there is no exception
to LLM services. Using a dedicated e-mail can let you have less information
leaks when the LLM services is breached, protecting your privacy and
possibility of phishing.

## Conclusion

When using LLM services, I urge you to use a dedicated e-mail address for
your privacy and accessibility of Internet services. By more five minutes
of registering, you protect your own area of boundary.
