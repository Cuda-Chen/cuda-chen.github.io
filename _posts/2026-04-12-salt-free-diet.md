## Introduction

Yeats ago, I started to make Sodium free dish when I made
my meals at home. Though you may think it is ridiculous,
I am going to show how to achieve this by using three
types of species: garlic, ginger, and chili pepper.

## Why You Should Try A Salt Free Dish?

Two reasons:

1. Decrease Sodium ingestion: many people nowadays
get their meals by dining out. In order to make you
buy their meals many times, the dealer usually add much
more Sodium (e.g., salt, MSG, etc.). After years, you start
to find your blood tension value is not good. What's
more, the chance of getting hypertension increases because
of your family or you are in a high-stress environment.
2. Enjoy another taste of food: as we are used to the
meals with Sodium, trying another taste of food without
any Sodium make a pleasure of your life: you get the original
taste of food.

## Garlic

I always rank #1 to garlic. With the spicy and its natural MSG,
it always makes me have a good appetite.

And for my garlic recipe:

- For each dish, I will put four to six cloves of garlic.
- For releasing the component that are good to your health,
I chop into piece and put for 10-15 minutes.

## Ginger

If you consider the garlic gives you bad breath after you finish
your meal, you can try ginger. It always fuse its mild spicy taste
with your meal.

For my ginger recipe:

- I will use the size of Distal phalanx of my thumb finger for each dish.
- Just like garlic, I chop into piece and put for 10-15 minutes.

## Chili Pepper

For the last one, chili pepper gives you a great appetite, though
you should always be aware of its spiciness (it will make you have
no appetite it you put too much).

For my chili pepper recipe:

- Taking Cayenne [^1] as example, I will use at most half slice
for each dish. 
- Just like the two other spices I usually use,
I chop into slices and put for 10-15 minutes.

## Conclusion

In this post, I make a showcase of how to do your Sodium free dish
by using either garlic, ginger, or chili pepper. However, you may
have your own recipe beyond these three spices. For communication,
let's leave a comment of you Sodium free dish spice recipe!
