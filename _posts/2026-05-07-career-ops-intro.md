> Disclamier: Neither I get sponsored nor am the author of Career-Ops.
> All of my thoughts are based on my experience.

Recently, I spotted a repo called Career-Ops [^1].
It says it can automate the job searching process, and I think it is totally
suitable for me as I always seeking for any opportunities. What's more,
I have some Google Gemini credit which I sometimes use it. So anyway,
I give Career-Ops a shot.

Surprisely, it not only helps to find some jobs that I have a hard time
to find, but also exhibits a new era of job seeking to me, which I
write this post for both record and to force myself to familiarize as
in the last month, everyone are stoked to use this tool for extraordinary
performance of job seeking.

## Characteristics

Career-Ops, in my post writing period, provides the following characteristics:

- Auto-pipeline
- 6-Block Evaluation
- Interview Story Bank
- Negotiation Scripts
- ATS PDF Generation
- Portal Scanner
- Batch Processing
- Dashboard TUI
- Human-in-the-Loop
- Pipeline Integrity

## My Experience

I list the subset I have used for checking its performance.

### Gemini CLI Integration

Because I have some Gemini credit and not familiar with any
openClaw-like tools, Career-Ops provide a native Gemini
CLI integration is a bit plus for me: I just need to install
Gemini CLI, then clone Career-Ops, go into the directory, and
execute Gemini CLI. After these, the magical happens.

### Job Seeking

I will say this will be the #1 feature to me (there is the other
#1 feature which I will talk later). It not only recommends
you the jobs with at least 4 out of 5, but also searches jobs
on various places then make the recommendation, which is a
tedious and repetative job if you are not familiar with
web scrapers.

### Resume Generation

Even though I have a LaTeX format resume and get above
90/100 score of ATS scan, I still think
it might be works as the generated resume remarks the keywords
of my ability.

I will give 3 out of 5.

### Job Evaluation

Just like job seeking feature, I rank this as the other #1
feature as it can make some evaluations so that you have quickly
have an impression of your job. What's more, it gives you
a hint of preparing interviews and salary.

## Future Aspects

I have tried for a few days, and Career-Ops is totally amazing!
The above listed features helps me a lot, and I can't wait
to use other features.

Stay tuned for my usage of trying other features.
